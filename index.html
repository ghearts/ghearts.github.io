Grateful Hearts Foundation offers a complete athlete management service.

We are committed to providing basic need and support for underprivileged children through the building of close healthy relationships
to mentor and guide them in order to help them achieve their
full potential as productive adults.

Grateful Hearts is based in Accra, Ghana.
